custom commissions are open. whether you have a specific vision or just know the feeling you want on your wall, reach out and we'll figure it out together.

how it works

01. send a message with your vision, size, budget, and where the piece will live.
02. ellie follows up with questions and a quote. a 50% deposit holds your spot.
03. the work is made. you receive progress photos along the way.
04. final payment on completion. shipped or available for local pickup.
